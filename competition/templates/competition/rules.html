Eligibility

Anyone can apply and participate in the challenge.

Competition Rules

Monitor Only Focus: Participants must look only at the screen. Looking at the keyboard is not allowed.

Touch Typing Required: Use all ten fingers in the Home Row position (ASDF-JKL;).

No External Aids: Auto-correct, predictive text, or third-party tools are strictly forbidden.

Accuracy Rule: Minimum 92% accuracy is required for a valid score.

Standard Hardware: Only organizer-provided QWERTY keyboards may be used.

Scoring: Rankings are based on Net WPM (Gross Speed âˆ’ Error Penalties).

Time Limit: Each participant gets exactly 3 minutes (or configured time).

Tie-Breaker: Higher accuracy wins if Net WPM is the same.

Single Attempt: Only one attempt is allowed unless there is a genuine hardware failure.

Any violation of the rules may lead to immediate disqualification.
